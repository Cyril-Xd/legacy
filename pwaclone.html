Progressive Web Apps and cloning
Progressive Web Apps are a special kind of
app that can be installed on a chromebook
even when apps are not allowed. It is very
easy to make one, I will not detail it here
as google is a thing. This website is built
as both a PWA and a clone.
A clone is a simple copy hosted on a different
url. Many websites can be cloned. All you do
is open the site, open dev tools, and go to
sources and save and rehost all the files there.
Some modification may be needed.
